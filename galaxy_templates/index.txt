# FSPS galaxy templates, Hubble-sequence order
Elliptical	old passive, delayed-tau
S0	lenticular, mostly old
Sa	early spiral
Sb	spiral
Sc	late spiral
Sd	very late spiral, const SFR
Irregular	blue dwarf/irregular
Starburst	young starburst, emission lines
Dusty_starburst	reddened starburst (Av~1.6)
Quiescent	red-and-dead SSP
